Top 5 Cyber Attacks

Posted in Blogs | CyberSec Diaries

Cyber attacks are malicious attempts to damage, disrupt, or gain unauthorized access to computer systems and networks. Understanding common cyber attacks helps users and organizations stay protected.

1. Phishing Attacks

Phishing attacks use fake emails or websites to trick users into sharing sensitive information such as passwords and banking details.

2. Malware Attacks

Malware includes viruses, worms, and trojans designed to harm systems or steal data.

3. Ransomware

Ransomware encrypts a victim’s data and demands payment to restore access.

4. Denial of Service (DoS)

DoS attacks flood servers with traffic, making services unavailable to users.

5. Password Attacks

Weak passwords can be guessed or cracked using brute-force or dictionary attacks.
